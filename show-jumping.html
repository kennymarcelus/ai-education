About the sport

"The bond between horse and rider is the foundation of every clear round."

Show jumping tests the athletic ability and obedience of horse and the skill of rider to jump a course of obstacles. Speed, accuracy, and trust define the discipline — from grassroots competition to the Olympic stage.
